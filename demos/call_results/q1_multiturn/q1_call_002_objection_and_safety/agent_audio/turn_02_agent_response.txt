We have come across instances where fraudsters are duping customers by claiming to be representatives of Lendingkart Finance Limited and also at times by using fake websites. Please beware of fraudsters while conducting your financial transactions - duly verify the authenticity of all communications (calls, emails, SMS, websites or advertisements) before making any payment or sharing any documents. Please be careful while opening links or attachments from unknown third parties. If anyone has asked you for upfront money, please do not pay and contact official support.
